ABOUT Me

Nancy DeVille

While a reporter at The Tennessean, her work earned her first place awards for Best News Reporting from the Tennessee Press Association. She's also a journalism educator who has taught workshops and university courses. The Bay Area native studied history at Fisk University in Nashville. Nancy received her Master of Journalism degree from the University of California, Berkeley. Nancy is currently a producer at Youth Radio.
