About

With a over a decade of journalism experience, Nancy has done work for several media outlets including NPR, USA Today, The Tennessean and the San Francisco Chronicle. While a reporter at The Tennessean, her work earned her first place awards for Best News Reporting from the Tennessee Press Association. She's also a journalism educator who has taught workshops and university courses.

The Bay Area native studied history at Fisk University in Nashville. Nancy received her Master of Journalism degree from the University of California, Berkeley. Nancy is currently a producer at Youth Radio.
